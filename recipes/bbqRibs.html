BBQ RIBS

Fall off the bones ribs, goes great with any source of carbs

When you hear the word BBQ Ribs, what comes to mind? You think of that sweet and savory sauce perfectly
layered on right? Well, this isn't that. This recipe is BBQ Ribs, but ISLAND STYLE!

A delicious recipe that's been marinated into the ribs and cooked to perfection over the grill, this is sure to be a
crowd pleaser at your next party or gathering! Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Rack of ribs
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 cup of vinegar
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 white onion

Steps

  1. Cup the rack of ribs into two parts
  2. Place ribs in tupperware (must be able to close lid)
  3. Pour in 1 cup on soy sauce and 1 cup of vinegar
  4. Pour 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  5. Pour 1 tbsp of black pepper
  6. Slice 1/2 of an onion and add
  7. Cover tupperware and give it a slight shake to cover ribs
  8. Place in refridgerator. For best results, leave overnight
