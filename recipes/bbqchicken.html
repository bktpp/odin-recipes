Taste so good, make you wanna

Description

This simple recipe is perfect when you are running late and don't know what to bring to the party!

Juicy chicken soaked in marination and grilled, will practically fall off the bone. You can never go wrong with a little BBQ Chicken!

Ingredients

  • Uncooked chicken
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 cup of vinegar
  • 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 white onion

Steps

  1. Place chicken in tupperware (must be able to close lid)
  2. Pour in 1 cup on soy sauce and 1 cup of vinegar
  3. Pour 1 tbsp of garlic powder
  4. Pour 1 tbsp of black pepper
  5. Slice 1/2 of an onion and add
  6. Cover tupperware and give it a slight shake to cover chicken
  7. Place in refridgerator. For best results, leave overnight
